## Summary

Chaoyue He is a researcher at Alibaba-NTU Global e-Sustainability CorpLab (ANGEL), Singapore. Lead author of "Harness Engineering for Language Agents" which proposes the CAR framework and HarnessCard reporting artifact.

## Key Contributions

### Harness Engineering for Language Agents (2026)
- **CAR Framework**: Control, Agency, Runtime decomposition of harness layer
- **HarnessCard**: Lightweight reporting artifact for agent configurations
- **Visibility Gap**: Audit of 63 works showing harness innovations often hidden
- **Key argument**: Many "agent gains" are harness-sensitive, not purely model-driven
